Red Thread — mathematically perfect circles

One match, six ways to weave it. Every panel is the 1999 final: United 2–1 Bayern, both goals in stoppage time (90+1′ Sheringham, 90+3′ Solskjær). The thread runs the clock — kickoff at the top, full time at the foot. Unlike wobbly spline approximations, the thread loops are drawn using SVG Arc commands, rendering mathematically perfect circles. Press Replay to watch them draw.
