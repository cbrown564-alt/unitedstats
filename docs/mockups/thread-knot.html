Red Thread — the knot is the night

One match, six ways to tie it. Every panel is the 1999 final: United 2–1 Bayern, both goals in stoppage time (90+1′ Sheringham, 90+3′ Solskjær). The thread runs the clock — kickoff at the top, full time at the foot — so the two goals fall almost on top of each other, late. The variations move knot size, shape, thread looseness and motion. Press replay to watch them tie.
