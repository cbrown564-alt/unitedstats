# CONTEXT — the shared picture

Working glossary from the restraint-pass clarity session (2026-06-29). The terms
and decisions Red Thread is built on, written down so they stop being unspoken.
Reference, not diary. When the durable parts settle they graduate into
`PRODUCT.md`; until then **this file wins where it conflicts with `PRODUCT.md`**
(conflicts flagged in §5).

*(Red Thread is the brand; "UnitedStats" is the repo/codebase name.)*

## 1. Who it's for — the nostalgist, as a mindset

The audience is the **nostalgist**, and the load-bearing move is that nostalgia
is **a mindset, not a demographic.** It isn't a segment you target; it's a frame
anyone can be invited into — including a 19-year-old who never saw George Best but
can still be brought close to him, to Munich, to the Treble. Broad appeal *if it
strikes the right note* — but always via the mindset, never by chasing a wider
segment.

Three modes of the one job:

- **Rediscover** a night you'd forgotten (the 2015 Europa League jolt).
- **Return** to one you lived, exactly as you felt it.
- **Get close to** one you never saw.

Explicitly **not** for: content creators (no connection; won't take off there);
the xG / spider-diagram stats-maker *as such*. Historians are served
**incidentally** — they'll dig regardless, so we don't curate for them.

Honest about reach: likely **a quiet, personal thing**, not a viral growth play.
The product shouldn't pretend otherwise.

## 2. What it's for — soul + foundation

Two beats of one experience, resting on a foundation:

- **Soul, beat 1 — the spark.** Trigger the nostalgic emotion. *Primary.* The
  pull, the reason you come.
- **Soul, beat 2 — the deepening.** Let the spark become curiosity and wonder — a
  new lens on an old memory, texture, a connection across eras that brings you
  *closer*. (Best & Ronaldo: both their most prolific season was their *fifth*,
  on one normalized scale — analysis that *deepened* the nostalgia instead of
  competing with it.) This is what makes it more than a memory jukebox.
- **Foundation — the complete, trustworthy record.** Why you *stay*, and why the
  nostalgia is *honest*: the jolt only lands because the record is whole, correct,
  and traceable, with more waiting behind it. The foundation is **load-bearing for
  the soul**, not grudging plumbing.

**The white space.** Greats get presented two ways — *peak moments in rich detail*
**or** *career in broad strokes* — with nothing in between. That empty middle
(connective, textured, cross-era) is Red Thread's real differentiator. The
deepening lives there.

**Working soul-sentence** *(wording not final; the image is settled, not the
words):*

> Red Thread is the line that runs through United's whole history — and through
> the life you've spent following it.

**Out of scope:** a *freshness loop* ("what did last night mean?") is **not** what
this is for (→ `/history-changed` becomes a cut/demote candidate). Nor an analytics
lab, a creator export tool, a debate-settler, or a growth play.

**Internal yardstick ≠ external pitch.** Everything above is the *internal*
decision yardstick. The launch/marketing pitch is a separate artifact, already
half-written in `docs/audience-evidence-base.md` §8.3 (the orphaned
stretfordend.co.uk story; free / open / honest). Don't conflate them.

## 3. The bar a surface must clear — *lens, not loom*

Three positions; only the third clears the bar:

- **View** — only hand-crafted; you consume what the author built. *Too narrow* —
  can't anticipate *your* connection, so the jolt only happens if the author
  pre-built that exact view.
- **Loom** — compare / build anything. *Too open* — the meaning is left for the
  user to supply; most won't; it emits cold permutations.
- **Lens** — the author designs a *thoughtful frame*; the user points it. **The
  meaning is baked into the lens**, so it pays off whatever the user brings to it.
  ✓

**Operational test:**

> Does the frame **guarantee meaning regardless of what the user points it at**, or
> does it make the user supply the meaning?

Normalized career-season comparison → guarantees it (lens). "Defenders on clean
sheets, not goals" / "per-90 vs total" → the authorial *judgment* is the value
(lens). "Any 2 of 969 players × any of 40 metrics × any axis" → outsources it
(loom). **Corollary:** one or two meaning-preserving knobs = lens; many free knobs
= loom. The craft is choosing the knobs so *every* setting still lands.

**This is respect for the user, made concrete.** Reliable, normalized data so the
thread *won't lead you down the garden path* (respect-via-reliability) **and** the
raw material to explore things the author never thought of, *not a box*
(respect-via-not-boxing-you-in). It's the internet; it's open data; it shouldn't
feel over-constrained. Care goes into **both** the data (real, reliable, traceable)
and the way you interact with it.

`PRODUCT.md` **Principle 2** ("compare like with like, on the right axis"), filed
there as a data-correctness rule, is hereby **promoted**: "the right axis" *is* the
authored lens. It is the design principle of the entire deepening layer — and where
era-awareness and metric-honesty get baked in.

**Restraint holds, one level up.** It now governs the *set of lenses* (a few, each
load-bearing, each earning its slot), not just the set of views. Build-anything
machinery still dies — not because comparison is cold, but because **self-serve
slicing abdicates authorship.** The magic was never in the machinery.

## 4. Surface verdicts (provisional — pending the surface-by-surface pass)

Produced by holding §3's bar against the live routes. Provisional: a yardstick
result, not yet executed cuts.

- **Pass (a moment on the thread):** `/match/[id]`, `/on-this-day`, `/surprise`.
- **Pass only *with* a Phase-3a rediscovery rail** (reference today): entity pages
  — `/player/[id]`, `/seasons/[season]`, `/opponent/[id]`.
- **Mixed — sorts by *role*, not surface:** `/questions` (the *Treble* passes;
  *decline since Ferguson* / *Europe* read as arguments, not moments — matches the
  Session-2 review); `/matches` (a *path* to a match = foundation; a facet
  playground = loom); `/explore` (a *doorway*, not a catalogue).
- **Reshape, don't cut:** `/compare` — from "rank any two on everything" → a small
  set of authored lenses (the Best/Ronaldo normalized template). The reason to
  exist it never had.
- **Cut / die:** the `/cut` self-serve fork builder, compare-anything,
  group-by-anything (no baked-in judgment); the freshness loop `/history-changed`
  (out of purpose).

  follow-on.**

## 6. What "done" means — the spark is the gate

**Done = depth, not reach.** The finish line is not "ready for a traffic spike";
it's "**the moment lands for a single nostalgist**," reliably. Reach is a
consequence, never the target.

- **The spark is the smallest concrete proof — and the gate.** First contact must
  make a fan feel something the live-score apps (FotMob, SofaScore) *structurally
  can't*. That fleeting feeling is the proof the thing has value at all, and it's
  the **necessary condition** for everything downstream: no spark → no return
  visit, no word of mouth, no community connection. FotMob et al. own the
  mindshare, so Red Thread has to feel **fundamentally different from the first
  moment** just to earn a fan's time.
- **The deepening is proof of *sustainable* value** — the authored lens that shows
  there's something genuinely new here, worth coming back to. But it only ever gets
  its turn *if the spark fires first.*

**Highest-leverage consequence:** the **front door *is* the gate** — its whole job
is to fire the spark within seconds — so engineering first-contact outranks the
slice-collapse mechanics. This sharpens §5's supersede: the front door's *entire
purpose* is the spark, not an answer/question field. And **Phase 3a (the
rediscovery engine) is elevated** — it is the spark generator, which is why it kept
surfacing as the real crux. Not a late-phase nicety; load-bearing for "done."
